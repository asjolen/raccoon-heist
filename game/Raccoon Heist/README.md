# 🦝 Raccoon Heist

**A 1–4 player co-op heist. You are raccoons. The shop is closed. Harold disagrees with
your business model.**

Every night, your gang of raccoons squeezes through the vent of a small corner shop
with one job: fill the shopping list. Eggs (don't drop them). Salami. Something
shiny. The cash register, eventually — that one's a two-raccoon carry.

The problem is Harold.

Harold owns this shop. Harold is asleep in the back room, in his boxers and
slippers, and Harold has had *enough*. As long as you can hear him snoring, you're
fine. When the snoring stops — freeze. When the flashlight comes around the shelf
row — hide. When Harold catches you, he doesn't hurt you. He picks you up, walks
to the front door, and throws you out of his shop. Your friends will have to let
you back in through the pet flap. They will be laughing too hard to do it quickly.

Get the loot to the vent before dawn. At 06:00 the alarm rings, the sun comes up,
and Harold starts his day — whether you're done or not.

## Features

- **1–4 players** — solo sneaking or full-gang chaos, with proximity voice chat when friends join
- **Physics loot**: fragile, heavy, awkward, and occasionally thrown at a friend
- **One angry man**: Harold patrols, sniffs, grumbles, and yeets. Learn his sounds
  or learn the pavement
- **Rising stakes**: greedier shopping lists every night, den upgrades between them
- **Dawn timer**: the shop gets brighter, your hiding spots get worse
- **Hats**: legally required

## Status

Early development. Harold exists and is furious. The shop is being built around him.
